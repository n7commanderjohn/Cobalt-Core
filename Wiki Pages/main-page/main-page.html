''Current game version: {{Version}}''

'''''[[Cobalt Core]]''''' is a roguelike deckbuilder centered around movement. All Ships can move left or right, vastly changing the way a battle plays out.

With over 200 [[:Category:Cards|cards]], 80 [[:Category:Artifacts|artifacts]], 8 playable [[:Category:Characters|characters]], 5 playable [[:Category:Ships|ships]], and [[:Category:Game|more]], you're sure to play for tens or hundreds of hours as you learn the intricacies of each [[:Category:Game Mechanics|mechanic]].
